NITK Gallery by MACD

National Institute of Technology Karnataka nestled between the green scapes of the western ghats and the sandy shores of the arabian sea has a lot to offer to any who ventures through it's campus. With this initiative, MACD, NITK tries to do justice to it's lush-green campus by being a platform to the moments that were frozen in time by NITK's bunch of very talented photographers.
